About

    Phishing is a type of cyberattack where attackers try to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information, such as passwords, credit card details, or personal data. They often do this by sending fake emails, messages, or creating websites that look like legitimate ones, but are designed to steal information. The goal of phishing is to deceive the victim into clicking a malicious link or providing confidential information.
Impact of Phishing on People
    There can be financial loss because phishing scams can trick people into providing sensitive financial information, such as their credit card numbers and it can lead to unauthorized transactions happening.
    Phishing Attacks can lead to identity theft, where attackers steal personal information such as Social Security Number and Addresses.
    Falling Victim to phishing can lead to stress, anxiety, and a loss of trust in online platforms, emails, and institutions.
